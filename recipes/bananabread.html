Banana Bread


Photo: Meredith Food Studios

This is a recipe for a delicious Banana Bread, with the added bonus of using up those overripe bananas on your counter. Great on its own, or as a treat to have with a cup of coffee or tea.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 1/3 cups mashed overripe bananas

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Lightly grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.

  2. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl and set aside.

  3. Beat brown sugar and butter with an electric mixer in a separate large bowl until smooth.

  4. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture until just combined. Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan.

  5. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 60 minutes. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely.
